Executive Summary

artifact:Chinese Martial Art

Social issue: the movie

the 14 Blades

the 14 Blades
talks about courage and indicates the skill and attraction of people in China to the Martial art. There are different types of martial art in this movie, which makes people addicted by it and see again and again.

Use specific issue of Kong Fu and Tai ji nowadays to impress how deep the martial art infects people. Then, using my own experience: Taekwondo. Comparing taewondo to Tai ji by my own feels. And from practice I can find my own value and decision which is more active and benefit for people. And I strongly encourage others to do martial art.

At last, find and suggest some points that need to improve. For there is no rule or law in China to protect and transmit martial art. It should be protected for our offsprings and futures.

Conclusion:every country owns its own cultures, whose their existence is a good demonstration of our culture. Martial art is one culture represents China. And we are proud of it.
